About

The OpenWIS Association AISBL is an International Non-profit Organization (Association Internationale Sans But Lucratif, or abbreviated AISBL) that aims to facilitate collaboration on the development, promotion and sharing of open source software for the exchange of global meteorological information.

OpenWIS is an Open source software for the exchange of global meteorological information and a sustainable solution for WIS compliance

Why choose the OpenWIS software?

  • WIS COMPLIANCE

    OpenWIS software is compliant with the WMO Information System (WIS) and other relevant standards. It addresses the needs of all WIS centres for data discovery, access and retrieval

  • SUSTAINABILITY

    OpenWIS software is underpinned by the OpenWIS Association ensuring long-term sustainability for WIS compliance

  • INTEROPERABILITY

    OpenWIS software integrates with NMHS's core systems using clearly defined interfaces.

Why join the OpenWIS Association?

  • COLLABORATION

    Collaborate on open source software for the exchange of global meteorological information.

  • EFFICIENCY

    Share costs of development and maintainence of solutions for exchange of global meteorological information with OpenWIS Association members.

  • STRATEGY

    Influence the direction of shared solutions for exchange of global meteorological information to support your operational activities
