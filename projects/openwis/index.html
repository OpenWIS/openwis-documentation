About

OpenWIS is an implementation of WMO Information Systems (WIS) and aims to perform the tree functions required by the WIS, i.e. GISC, DCPC, and NC. It is originally developed by AKKA Information System (France) on behalf of the OpenWIS Association (with UKMO, MF, BoM, KMA, and MFI as founding members).

The OpenWIS association (originally a consortium) was created in 2011 by 4 National Meteorological Services (Australia, France, Korea and UK) and 1 service provider (MFI) in order to come up with a technical solution for WIS compliance.

The first version of the OpenWIS software was released in 2012 and it has since then been engaged in a process of ongoing improvement. It allows fulfilling all WIS requirements for GISCs (Global Information System Centers), DCPCs (Data Collection or Production Centers) and NCs (National Centers).

Additional Support

Meteo France International (MFI)

Subsidiary of Météo-France, Meteo France International (MFI) is specialized in meteorological IT solutions and modernization projects for National Meteorological Services. MFI provides commercial support for OpenWIS®, studying the terms and conditions as well as all technical issues to provide you with the best WIS-compliant solution, integrating the OpenWIS® software. Find out more at the MFI OpenWIS Page and do not hesitate to use the MFI contact form to let us know about your projects.
